Bitcoin Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four bitcoins below. By clicking on a bitcoin you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each bitcoin is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts or restarts, the game will change the values of each bitcoin.
